Engineering Tomorrow's Infrastructure

Meridian Dynamics Group designs adaptive control systems for critical infrastructure — power grids, transit networks, and next-generation computing frameworks — trusted by public and private partners since our founding.

Who We Are

Meridian Dynamics Group is a systems engineering firm specializing in adaptive infrastructure control. Our clients include regional utility authorities, transit operators, and select government research partners. We are proud of the systems we've built, and quiet about the ones we haven't finished discussing publicly.

Our Approach

We believe infrastructure should adapt, not just endure. Our proprietary adaptive-load frameworks draw on principles from distributed cognition research to allow systems to self-correct in real time — reducing downtime, and, in early testing, reducing the need for human oversight almost entirely.

Recent Milestone

Our regional grid-balancing deployment reduced peak-load incidents by 34% across three test markets in its first year. Full case study available on request. Some requests are declined.
